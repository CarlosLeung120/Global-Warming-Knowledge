Effects of Global Warming

After talking about global warming itself and the causes of global warming, I am now going to talk about what happens after global warming has happened, the effects of global warming

Rising Sea level


So the first effect of global warming is the rise of sea level in the oceans, and the reason for that is because of the icebergs all around the world melting into water, causing the sea level to rise since there is more water, another affect after that is that some places around the world that are lowlands, and because sea level is rising, these lowlands will have a lot more problems of flooding after global warming has happened, also in the worst case, the lowlands can get lower than the sea level, causing the lowland to be gone forever.


Extreme weather

As the temperature rises, the weather will get more and more extreme and unstable, causing more intense storms, floods, and snowfalls to happen more occasionally. It also causes longer and more frequent drougts in some special areas.



Crops and Animals

When global warming happens, plants and crops grow less since it is harder for them to grow. Moreover, Animals may have to move to other areas in the earth to live since it may not be suitable for them to live. Also, water supply can decrease immensly because of the heat.



Health Issues arrise

When there is global warming, it can directly affect us humans. In urban areas, the amount of smog increases, causing a higher chance of getting asthma, heart diseases and lung cancer.
