What the government can do



After talking about what you can do to help the enviroment, there are also things that the government can do to help the enviroment.



Create more green spaces in cities


This is quite a good idea to improve global warming by first improving air pollution, and planting trees and creating a green space in the city can help with the air pollution in the city.



Promote retrofitting

You might have not heard of these things before but Retrofitting things is basically to use something old add new things to it and taking out old things, without buying or getting a whole new thing just because of a problem, this method can be used in vehicles, buidings and more, by using this method we can therefore use less matierials which therefore will not have a need of creating so many new things, and that can therefore reduce the amount of Co2 produced when making new things, which can help improve global warming. And that is why the government should start promoting that.
