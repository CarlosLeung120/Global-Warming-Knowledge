What factories and companies can do



So after taking about all the other things, its time to talk about our last topic, what can factories and companies do in order to help the enviroment.



Turn off lights, computers and other appliances when not in use


Well you might have heard of this before saying that you should do this in your daily life, well it also applies to factories too, when you are not using these items why not turn it off, it doesn't do much unless you are using the item, so just turn the lights and fans off when not in use.



Encourage teleconference than physical conference


Nowadays, people oftenly use the computer to do things like work, play and other things, so why not also use it more oftenly for meetings, doing a teleconference meeting is faster, can be done more easily, uses less paper, and people don't have to travel to a specific workplace just to have a meeting, and all these things added up, you can reduce the use of energy by a lot more.
